PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: June 4, 2026
The short version: Monkey Shuttle does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information about you. All game data stays on your device.

1. What This Policy Covers

This Privacy Policy applies to the Monkey Shuttle mobile application ("the app") developed by Aaron Johnsen ("we", "our"). By using the app, you agree to the terms described below.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Monkey Shuttle does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Account credentials or login information
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, advertising IDs, device serial numbers)
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based location, or otherwise)
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or other device permissions
  • Browsing history or activity outside the app
  • Crash reports or analytics
  • Health or fitness data
  • Financial or payment information

3. Local Game Data

The app stores the following information locally on your device using your operating system's local storage mechanism:

  • Game progress (high scores, run history, total runs)
  • Earned in-game bananas (currency) and purchased upgrades
  • Daily streak count and last play date
  • Daily mission progress
  • Preferences (sound enabled, vibration enabled)
  • Active boost timers (Banana Doubler expiration)

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, including our own. We do not have access to it. You can delete all of this data at any time using the "Reset Save Data" button in the in-app Options menu, or by deleting and reinstalling the app.

4. Third-Party Services

Monkey Shuttle does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics platforms, or social media services. The app does not contain ads. The app does not require an internet connection to play.

5. Children's Privacy

Monkey Shuttle is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because we do not collect personal data, the standard rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and similar laws are automatically satisfied:

  • Right to access: We have no data about you to provide.
  • Right to deletion: Your in-app data can be deleted from the Options menu.
  • Right to opt out of sale: We do not sell or share any data with anyone.
  • Right to data portability: Your game data is stored in plain format on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or in legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated through an update to the app and reflected by a new "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
