About SENsei
先生 · 市場 · 知恵
Teacher · Market · Wisdom

SENsei is a daily stock screening tool built by Aaron Johnsen. The name is a nod to the sen in Johnsen — and to 先生, the Japanese word for teacher or master, someone who came before and knows more.

Every day, SENsei uses AI (Claude with web search) and free public market data (Yahoo Finance) to surface two stocks with notable momentum characteristics — one positive, one negative. No subscriptions. No paywalls. No paid data feeds.

This is not investment advice. SENsei is an educational and entertainment tool. It identifies observable market signals, not trading recommendations. Always do your own research and consult a licensed financial advisor before making any investment decision.

SENsei is not registered with the SEC or any securities regulatory authority. It operates under the general publisher exclusion for educational financial content.
